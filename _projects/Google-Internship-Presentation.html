During my Summer 2019 internship at Google, I took part in the Intern Talks program at the Google office in Kitchener-Waterloo, Ontario, Canada. This gave me the opportunity to give a ten to fifteen minute presentation, on a topic of my choice, to Google engineers and interns. I entitled the presentation “Treasures in Retrogame Archeology”, and I chose to present about some of the lessons that I had learned studying retro videogames in the retrogames special topic class offered by the University of Calgary.
